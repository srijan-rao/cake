Wedding Cake Information


Create Your Dream Cake with BAKE IT!
At BAKE IT, we understand that your wedding cake is a centerpiece of your celebration and a symbol of your love story. Here’s what we offer:
Custom Designs: We specialize in bespoke wedding cakes tailored to your vision. Whether you dream of a classic, modern, rustic, or whimsical design, our talented team will bring your ideas to life.
Flavor Selection: Choose from a wide array of flavors, from traditional vanilla and rich chocolate to unique options like lavender lemon or almond raspberry. You can even mix and match tiers for a delightful experience!
Multiple Tiers: Our wedding cakes can be designed in various tiers to serve your guests beautifully. We can guide you on sizing based on your guest list and preferences.
Tastings: Book a tasting session to sample our delicious flavors and discuss design options with our team. This is your chance to find the perfect combination for your big day!
Delivery & Setup: We offer reliable delivery and setup services to ensure your cake arrives safely and looks stunning at your venue.
Allergies & Dietary Needs: We cater to various dietary preferences, including gluten-free, vegan, and more. Just let us know your requirements, and we’ll create a delicious cake for everyone to enjoy.
Consultation: Schedule a consultation with our team to discuss your ideas, inspirations, and any questions you may have. We’re here to make your cake planning as stress-free as possible!
